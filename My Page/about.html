Established in 2012, New Ultimate IT Institute has been a hub for computer enthusiasts and learners in Kalika Municipality. We take pride in being the very first computer institute in our area, and our primary mission is to make you proficient in all things related to computers and technology.

Our dedicated instructors are experts in their fields, ensuring that you receive top-notch education. Our well-equipped classrooms provide the ideal environment for hands-on learning experiences. Our course offerings are comprehensive, covering a wide spectrum of computer-related subjects, from computer basics to advanced topics.
